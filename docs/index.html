BEAT  BLITZ

A rhythm‑charged platformer fusion — jump, jam, repeat.

Beat Blitz marries the pulse‑pounding jumps of Geometry Dash with the colour‑coded note precision of Guitar Hero. Leap between platforms that glow red, green, or blue; hit the matching key in time and the soundtrack blooms. Miss the colour or the beat and you’ll hear wrong notes, drum dropouts, or total silence as the level scrolls on. Each stage is generated from a MIDI track, so the level is the music — play it tight and you’ll perform the song.

Points flow from perfect colour‑synced jumps, long streaks ignite ON FIRE multipliers, and every successful run is scored, starred, and tracked so you can chase that three‑star perfect. A mis‑timed leap costs you — fifty points and a sour chord — but you can always restart, tap into the beat, and try for perfection.
